Scholarship Programme

We believe financial constraints should never limit a child's access to quality education. Our scholarship programme opens doors for deserving students.

Scholarship Programme

Opening Doors for Bright Minds

At HLIS, we firmly believe that every child deserves access to quality, faith-based education regardless of their family's financial circumstances. Our scholarship programme is built on this conviction.

We identify academically talented and deserving students and offer support that enables them to receive a full HLIS education — developing them into future leaders, thinkers, and contributors to society.

Merit-Based Awards

Scholarships are awarded based on academic performance, character, and demonstrated potential.

Need-Based Support

We also consider family financial circumstance to ensure no deserving child is turned away.

Annual Application

New scholarship positions are available every academic year — applications reviewed each term.

What We Offer

Types of Scholarship Awards

Several levels of financial support to meet different needs and recognise various forms of excellence.

Full Scholarship

Complete tuition coverage for exceptionally talented students who demonstrate outstanding academic excellence and leadership qualities.

Partial Scholarship

50% tuition reduction for qualified students — bridging the financial gap while encouraging continued academic striving.

Excellence Award

Granted to existing students who achieve top results at the end of the academic year as recognition and encouragement.

Application Process

How to Apply

The process is simple and accessible so no deserving child misses an opportunity.

  • Submit an Enquiry

    Contact us via the Contact page or visit the school office to express interest in the scholarship programme.

  • Complete the Application Form

    Fill in the official scholarship application form, including academic records and a brief personal statement.

  • Attend an Assessment

    Shortlisted candidates are invited for an academic assessment and brief interview with the admissions team.

  • Receive a Decision

    Successful applicants are notified within two weeks. Award details and conditions are provided in writing.

Eligibility Criteria

To be considered, applicants must meet the following requirements:

Minimum grade average of 70% or B in previous academic year

Good conduct record and positive teacher recommendations

Demonstrated financial need (for need-based awards)

Applications accepted for students in Primary 2 through Secondary 3

Must pass the scholarship assessment and interview stage
