About HLIS

Nurturing Minds, Building Character

At Humble Light International School, we believe in nurturing students to excel both academically and spiritually. Our mission is to provide a godly atmosphere where students thrive in their studies while growing in faith.

Since 2013, we have remained committed to academic excellence, character development, and holistic growth — producing well-rounded individuals prepared for tomorrow's world.

Comprehensive Curriculum

From Reception to Secondary — a structured academic journey with strong foundations at every level.

Godly Environment

Christian values woven into every aspect of school life, shaping hearts and character daily.

Holistic Development

Beyond academics — sports, arts, coding, and leadership to develop the whole child.
