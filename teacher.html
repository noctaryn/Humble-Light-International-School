Our Educators

Meet the visionary leader and passionate team of educators who make Humble Light International School a place where every child can truly thrive.

2013

Founder & Vision Holder

Menkiti Chude

Director · Humble Light International School
"Every child is a light waiting to shine. Our calling is simply to create the environment where that light grows brightest."

Menkiti Chude is the founder and vision holder of Humble Light International School, established in Lagos in 2013 with a single, unwavering conviction — that quality, faith-centred education should be accessible to every child, regardless of background.

Driven by a deep personal faith and a passion for child development, he built HLIS from the ground up as an institution where academic excellence and spiritual grounding are not competing priorities but inseparable ones. Under his leadership, the school has grown from a small community initiative into a thriving institution serving hundreds of families across Lagos.

His vision for HLIS extends beyond examination results — it is the formation of young people of integrity, purpose, and impact who will shape the future of Nigeria and beyond.

The Vision

What Drives Humble Light

Three convictions that have guided every decision since the school's founding.

Faith as Foundation

Every programme, classroom, and relationship at HLIS is shaped by Christian values — not as a subject taught, but as a culture lived.
