Our Story

The Beginning of Humble Light

Founded in 2013, Humble Light International School was established with the vision of providing quality education rooted in strong Christian values. From the very beginning, the school has remained committed to a single purpose: raising children who are academically excellent and spiritually grounded.

What started as a small community school has grown into a thriving institution that serves hundreds of families — bringing the best of holistic education to every child who walks through our doors.

Today, HLIS continues to grow, innovate, and inspire — driven by the belief that every child carries a God-given potential worth nurturing.

Academic Levels

A Comprehensive Educational Journey

From the earliest years to senior secondary — HLIS provides a structured, nurturing academic path at every stage.

Reception

A warm, playful environment that fosters early socialization, language development, and cognitive growth — the perfect first step into school life.

Preschool

Focused on early literacy, numeracy, creativity, and emotional growth. We lay the foundation for a love of learning that lasts a lifetime.

Primary

Building strong academic foundations across core subjects while encouraging curiosity, critical thinking, and character development in every student.

Secondary

Rigorous academics and leadership training prepare students for WAEC, NECO, and future success in higher education and beyond.
