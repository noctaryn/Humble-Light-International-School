Frequently Asked Questions

What age does HLIS admit from?

We accept children from age 3 (Reception) through to Senior Secondary School.

When does the academic year begin?

Our academic year begins in September. Admission applications are accepted year-round.

Do you offer after-school care?

Yes, we offer supervised after-school care until 5:30 PM for parents who need the extra time.

Is there a school bus service?

We partner with trusted transport providers for safe school bus services across select Lagos routes.
